# Privacy Policy

## Overview
Signboard is a simple text display app that stores your text locally on your device.

## Data Collection
- **No data collection**: Signboard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.
- **Local storage only**: All text you enter is stored only on your device using Android's SharedPreferences.
- **No internet**: The app does not connect to the internet or any external servers.
- **No third-party services**: We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party SDKs.

## Data Retention
All data is deleted when you uninstall the app.
